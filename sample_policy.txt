Section 1: Coverage
This policy provides coverage for accidental damage, fire, and theft of insured property.

Section 2: Exclusions
The policy does not cover damage caused by war, nuclear events, or intentional acts.

Section 3: Natural Disasters
Coverage for floods and earthquakes is included only if specified in the policy schedule.

Section 4: Claims Process
To file a claim, the insured must notify the insurer within 7 days of the incident
and submit required documents including proof of loss.

Section 5: Policy Limits
The maximum coverage amount is subject to limits mentioned in the policy schedule.
